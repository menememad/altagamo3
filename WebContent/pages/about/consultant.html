Who we are

It is our great pleasure to introduce ourselves as a specialized design and consulting group in the field of reinforced concrete design, working in the design and consulting business since 1962, and having our own consulting office since 1987.

Integrated design projects in other fields are also accomplished through our consulting office.

The staff of our office includes:

  3. Dr. - Ing. Khaled Hilal Riad
